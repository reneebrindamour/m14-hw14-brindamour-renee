About Me

I reside in southeast Michigan, sharing my home with my son, a loyal dog, and two affectionate cats. My life is enriched by a diverse array of hobbies, which encompass mountain hiking, reading, fishing, photography, experimenting with new recipes, and exploring the world through travel.

I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in graphic design and media arts with a concentration in web design from Southern New Hampshire University. I graduated Summa Cum Laude, which is an honor that signifies "with highest honors." Furthermore, I also studied professional photography at the New York Institute of Photography.

Due to my outstanding academic achievements, I was nominated by the university and granted membership in one of their prestigious honor societies, known as the National Society of Leadership and Success. I successfully completed an intensive leadership training program that extended beyond the mere acquisition of leadership skills. The National Society of Leadership and Success places a strong emphasis on holding its members accountable in various areas, including leadership, integrity, work ethic, goal setting, high achievement, fostering positivity, and serving as a positive role model.

Over the past decade, I have amassed extensive experience as a freelance graphic designer. Furthermore, I have been an integral part of a marketing team where collaborative efforts were crucial. In this role, my duties involved creating diverse digital and print materials such as digital banners, social media content, marketing emails, company holiday cards, posters, infographics, signage, and icons. Additionally, I effectively administered the company's website utilizing WordPress, and I also executed customizations using HTML, CSS. JavaScript, and jQuery.

Prior to embarking on my design journey, I spent eight years as a realtor in the Ann Arbor, Michigan area, during which I garnered awards for my outstanding performance as a multi-million dollar real estate sales producer.

My extensive experience as an independent contractor over the years has honed my ability to be a proactive self-starter. Simultaneously, my tenure in collaborative team environments has underscored the vital significance of effective teamwork and collaboration. These experiences have instilled in me a unique blend of traits, as I am inherently motivated, brimming with energy, and fueled by boundless creativity.

I am currently working as a web designer for the University of Michigan - Michigan Medicine. Additionally, I am pursuing a Master's of Arts in Mass Communication, Web Design, and Online Communication from the University of Florida with an expected graduation year of May 2026.

Career Goals

I once aspired to be a web designer. In March 2024, I achieved this goal when I was hired as a web designer for the University of Michigan. However, I believe that success is not a destination but a journey.

My current goal is to secure a web design position that offers more creative freedom and development opportunities. Ultimately, I aspire to lead a team of creative designers and contribute to the growth of others by sharing my knowledge and experience in web design.
